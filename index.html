Equipment You Would Need

1. Yarn

Any yarn works, but I recommend using bright colors to make it easier for beginners. Acrylic yarn and medium-weight worsted yarn are perfect for starters!


2. Crochet Hook

The size of the crochet hook depends on the yarn you're using, but a 5mm or H-8 hook is a great starting point for most projects!


3. Scissors

You'll need a small, sharp pair of scissors to cut the yarn when you're done with a project. A thread cutter can also be handy for snipping small ends.


4. Tapestry Needle

A tapestry needle is essential for weaving in loose ends and finishing your crochet projects neatly.


5. Stitch Markers

Stitch markers are great for keeping track of your stitch counts or marking the beginning of rounds in circular crochet projects.


6. Measuring Tape

Measuring tape helps you keep track of the size of your project, whether it's a scarf, blanket, or sweater!
