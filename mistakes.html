Common Mistakes When Crocheting ⚠️

Learn how to avoid these beginner mistakes and improve your crochet skills!

1. Tension Issues 🙆‍♀️

Too tight or too loose tension can affect the size and shape of your project. Practice finding the right tension to keep your stitches even.

2. Skipping Stitches 🚫

Sometimes, it’s easy to skip a stitch when crocheting in the round. Always count your stitches to avoid mistakes!
