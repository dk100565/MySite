Tips & Tricks for Crochet Success ✨

Get better at crocheting with these helpful tips and tricks!

Tip 1: Use a Stitch Marker 🧷

Stitch markers are great for keeping track of your starting point, especially when working in the round.

Tip 2: Count Your Stitches 🔢

Always count your stitches to make sure you’re not increasing or decreasing by accident. Keep a stitch counter or a notebook handy!
